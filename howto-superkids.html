Second Grade SuperKids Login

This page should help you log into the SuperKids Readers Section of the website. Please see the steps below.

Step 1: Login Page

In the Internet browser of your choice, go to https://teachers.rowlandreading.org/teachers/tp2Index.html#login?ReturnUrl=%2f

Your email address should be your login. The password was set up earlier in the year. An email was sent earlier in the year (around March 26, 2020) with specific instructions on how to set this up.

Step 2: SuperKids Online Fun!

Under the SuperKids Online Fun logo, there are three (3) green buttons. Choose your device (for this example, we are choosing the button labeled "for desktop/laptop and Chromebook").

Step 3: Loading

There will be a short "loading" screen...

Step 4: Click To Play

Select the "Click to Play" button.

Step 5: Pick Your Password

One of the icons on this page will be the password that your child chose. Select one of the icons.

If your child doesn't remember which icon they previously chose, please let the teacher know.

Step 6: Read

Select the "Read" icon.

Step 7: Choose Your SuperKids Reader

The tan SuperKids book is the current book we are working with. Select the book.

Step 8: Take Off!

That's it! You should be able to scroll through the pages using the back and forth icons on the left and right.

We hope this helps. If you are still having issues, please contact your teacher.
